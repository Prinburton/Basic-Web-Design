Types of Cheese

There are about 2,000 types of cheese across the globe. Each type of cheese is unique in its flavor, texture, and other characteristics.

Cheddar Cheese

Cheddar cheese is very popular in the United States. This cheese can range from creamy to sharp. Its color can run between a natural white to a pumpkin orange. Cheddar's texture changes as it ages. The older it is the more drier and crumbly it is.

Colby Cheese

Colby Cheese is a mild, semi hard cheese. This cheese is similar to cheddar cheese but it is softer in texture. It is also less tangy in taste. Colby Cheese is often used in sandwiches and snacks. It is usually blended with Monterey Jack cheese to make Colby Jack Cheese.
